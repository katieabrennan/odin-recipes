Breakfast Noodles

Description

Picture a fry-up on top of a bowl of instant noodles, and you'll get an idea of what this is all about. A cup of tea is optional, but highly recommended.

Ingredients

  • 3 mushrooms
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 spring onion
  • 100g instant noodles
  • 4 rashers streaky bacon
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • vegetable oil

Method

  1. Thinly slice your mushrooms, chop your tomato in half and thinly slice your spring onion.
  2. Cook your noodles according to packet instructions. Add the seasonings from the packet and soy sauce. Give it a good mix.
  3. Meanwhile, fry your bacon until crispy then set aside. Now, fry up your egg with the tomato and mushrooms in the same pan and set them aside, too.
  4. Pour the noodles into your favourite bowl with the tomato, egg, bacon, mushrooms and spring onion. Tuck in.
